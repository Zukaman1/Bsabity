Our Services

What We Manufacture

High-durability wood and metal fabrications tailored to residential and commercial projects.

Metal & Wooden Beds

We manufacture premium double beds, single beds, and bunk beds. Choose between heavy-duty structural metal tubing or solid treated woods (Mahogany, Teak) for maximum noise-free frame stability.

Tables & Office Desks

Custom-fabricated wooden dining tables, executive office desks, boardroom tables, and coffee tables. Crafted with precision wood joins and protected with high-gloss or matte varnishes.

Wardrobes & Cabinets

Space-saving sliding or hinge-door bedroom wardrobes, file cabinets, book shelves, and floating TV stands. Built with heavy-duty metal slider tracks and soft-close cabinet hinges.

Premium Kitchen Cabinets

Turnkey kitchen design and installation. We manufacture water-resistant modular wood panels, acrylic doors, deep utility drawers, and quartz/granite countertop frames.

Chairs & Seating Sets

Durable office ergonomic chairs, restaurant chairs, living room sofas, and bar stools. Features triple-welded steel bases and high-density memory foam cushioning covered in premium textiles.

Doors, Windows & Outdoor

Manufactured heavy security wood doors, sliding windows, and weather-proof teak wood patio dining sets. Engineered to withstand the outdoor Rwandan sunshine and torrential rains.

Need a Custom-Sized Solution?

Don't worry if your room has non-standard dimensions. Bsabity specialises in fully custom CAD-engineered drawings and bespoke manufacturing. Our artisans will visit your home/office in Rwanda for precise site measurements.
