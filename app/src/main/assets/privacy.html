Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Read on to understand how we protect your personal and custom design data.

1. Information Collected

We collect standard personal details necessary for processing custom project inquiries and delivery. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and physical coordinates in Rwanda when you submit custom measurement requests.

2. Cookies & Analytics

Our platform uses local cookies and web storage to store preferences such as your language choice (EN/RW) and theme (Dark/Light). We do not track you across other websites or sell your browsing history to advertisers.

3. Firebase & Security

We utilize secure cloud database solutions, including Google Firebase, to hold and manage product items, inquiry logs, and admin credentials. All database interactions are protected by Firebase Security Rules and advanced SSL/TLS encryption standard practices.

4. Contact Form Data

Information submitted through the "Get in Touch" forms is sent directly to our workshop managers via Firebase. This data is strictly utilized to contact you back regarding your inquiry and is never shared with third-party marketing entities.

5. User Rights

You have the complete right to request a copy of the communication history we have on record for your email, or request complete removal of your inquiry files from our database. To do so, please drop an email directly to our support desk.

6. Contact Information
