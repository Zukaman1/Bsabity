Pioneering Design

Our Journey & Craft

Bsabity Furniture Workshop was founded in 2012 by an ambitious team of local artisans determined to redefine the local manufacturing industry. What started as a humble carpentry shop with a single workbench has evolved into a premier design and metal fabrication factory in Rwanda.

We recognized early on that consumers were forced to choose between cheap, easily-damaged imports or unaffordable custom work. Bsabity was established to fill this gap: providing local, custom-manufactured, exceptionally durable metal and wooden furniture direct to customers at factory-direct pricing.

Today, we employ over 15 skilled craftspeople, welding specialists, and designers. We use local sustainable timbers and premium industrial-strength steel to ensure that every table, bed frame, wardrobe, and cabinet we sell is an investment that lasts for generations.

Our Mission

To design and manufacture durable, exquisite furniture that enriches Rwandese homes and corporate spaces with lasting comfort.

Our Vision

To be the leading indigenous furniture maker in East Africa, recognized for unmatched engineering, design, and integrity.

Values

What Drives Us

Every piece of furniture we manufacture complies with these three fundamental standards.

Integrity of Timber

We do not cut corners with cheap wood veneers. We select verified, solid hardwood timbers that withstand tropical humidity and high mechanical stresses.

Exceptional Joinery

Our joints are hand-constructed and triple-reinforced. From precise mortise-and-tenon wood joins to professional-grade metal MIG welds, our engineering ensures zero wiggles.

Sustainable Sourcing

We support responsible forestry in Rwanda and East Africa. 100% of our wood supply is sustainably sourced and certified by environmental authorities.
